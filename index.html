Task Prioritization & Scheduling

Introduction: This tool helps you **prioritize tasks** based on importance and urgency, then schedules them efficiently.

How to Use:

  • Click "+ Add Task" to enter a new task.
  • Answer each prioritization question honestly.
  • Click "Prioritize & Schedule" to rank your tasks.
  • Review the prioritized list and adjust due dates if needed.
